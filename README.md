# Survival Pong

A twist on the classic Pong — your goal isn't to score, it's to **survive as long as possible**.

## How to Play

Keep the ball in play against an increasingly difficult AI opponent. The longer you last, the higher your score.

## Worlds

The game features **4 polygon worlds**, each with a unique arena shape that changes how the ball bounces and how you must move:

1. **Square** — the classic rectangular court
2. **Triangle** — tight corners and unpredictable angles
3. **Pentagon** — wider arena with more wall surfaces
4. **Hexagon** — the ultimate survival challenge

## Play

Open `index.html` in your browser — no install required.
